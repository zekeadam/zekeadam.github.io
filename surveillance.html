Surveillance
Surveillance is an interactive three dimensional stereo projection where one can chose from thousands of live surveillance cameras to observe. The video feed of the selected stream will be recorded and presented in a way where the consequent frames of the last minute build up a virtual cube which the viewer can manipulate by walking around in front of the screen. This unconventional way of visualization allows exploration and omnipresence of different time slices.

Made in collaboration with Tobias Gremmler. (syncon-d.com)
