Ephemera
"Ephemera" is an interactive exhibition where time intertwines the individual with the collective. Upon entering the exhibition, visitors are greeted by a captivating array of large digital timers, each meticulously displaying the age of individual participants in years, months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds. As visitors input their birth times, their personal timers seamlessly integrate into a dynamic grid, forming a mesmerizing visual tapestry that symbolizes the collective passage of existence.

The ambient soundscape envelops visitors in a contemplative atmosphere, with an ever-evolving, generative, meditative drone that is modulated by the movement of the timers. This drone serves to deepen the immersive experience, inviting introspection and reflection on the profound interplay of individual lives within the greater fabric of time.

Against this backdrop, visitors are encouraged to ponder the transient nature of existence and the universal journey shared by all humanity. Each timer represents a unique story, a singular thread woven into the rich tapestry of human experience. As visitors observe the convergence of countless timers, they are reminded of the interconnectedness of lives and the fleeting beauty found in each passing moment.

"Ephemera" serves as a poignant reminder of the fragility and preciousness of life, urging visitors to cherish the present and contemplate the significance of their own existence within the grand symphony of time. Through its interactive and evocative design, the exhibition offers a profound exploration of mortality, memory, and the enduring legacy of the human spirit.
