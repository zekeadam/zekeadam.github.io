SUM
According to a saying, every human being is the sum of the people who spend the most time with. This thought provided the bases for my work. In the XXI. century, the human relationships and our habits have changed. That's because to the fact, that our lives have accelerated and digital telecommunications has become commonplace. We take it natural that we can reach anyone at any time. We also take it for granted that we share the moments of our lives with our acquaintances through the World Wide Web. My work reflects on these things by visualising people constructed by their social networks which was photographed in their living spaces trough a special light paingting technique.
