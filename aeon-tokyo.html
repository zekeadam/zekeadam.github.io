AEON - Tokyo
"AEON" is a projection mapping installation designed for the Tokyo Lights Festival, set against the backdrop of the Meiji Memorial Picture Gallery building. The artwork combines fractal patterns and morphing faces to explore the complexities of human identity and experience.

The visuals are accompanied by a poem that delves into the contradictions of the human condition: our desire for connection amid isolation, the tension between progress and tradition, and the quest for harmony despite the discord we often create. The constantly changing faces in the projection reflect these themes, symbolizing the fluid and ever-evolving nature of who we are.
