Hybrid Space
Hybrid Space is an experimental project involving virtual reality headsets in conjunction with depth sensing cameras to create a unique experience where the user's perception of itself and its surroundings are replaced with the real-time data provided by the system.

Made in collaboration with Tobias Gremmler. (syncon-d.com)

More information about the project is available in the article by VrScout.com:
vrscout.com/projects/an-out-of-body-vr-experience/
